Healthy choices at Kapiti College Canteen

Our aim is to offer a variety of healthy choices at a reasonable cost. In addition to the menu we endeavour to have new items daily on a blackboard outside the canteen. We encourage pre-ordering of lunch, so not be disap- pointed when the queue is long and demand exceeds supply.

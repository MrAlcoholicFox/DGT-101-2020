Here is my recipe for making chocolate chip cookies

Ingredients

  • 250g butter
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 cup caster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla escense
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 1/2 cups self raising flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips (you can use crushed chocolate)

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees celsius
  2. Grease two baking trays with non stick baking paper (You can use butter if you have special trays)
  3. Beat the butter, brown sugar, caster sugar and vanilla together until well combined
  4. Add the eggs and beat until fully mixed
  5. Add the Flour and salt and mix until well combined before adding the chocolate chips
  6. Grab a little bit of the dough and roll it into a ball before putting it on the baking tray
  7. Repeat until all of the dough is used
  8. Bake for around 15 mins

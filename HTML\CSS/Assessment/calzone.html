Here is my recipe for making mediocre Calzone

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1 Cup Flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp Yeast
  • 1/2 tsp Sugar
  • 1/3 Cup Warm Water

Filling

  • 60g Tasty cheese
  • 1/2 of a capsicum, diced
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 4T tomato paste

Method

Making the dough

  1. Put the sugar, water and yeast into a small bowl or measuring cup
  2. Put the flour and the salt into a large bowl
  3. Once the yeast mixture has risen and is frothy pour it into the bowl with the Flour
  4. Mix together until they are combined and then knead it on a floured surface. (It should look like the pizza dough)
  5. When you are happy with your dough then leave it to rise with a tea towel over the bowl

Making the filling

  1. Get yourself a large bowl
  2. Mix all of the Ingredients together (make sure to chop the ones that need chopping)
  3. Leave in the fridge until you are ready to actually make the calzones

It should look like this when you are done

Making the calzone

  1. Put your oven at 220 degrees celsius
  2. Seperate your dough into as many calzones that you want
  3. Grab one and roll it out on your floured surface
  4. roll it into a small circle
  5. Place a small amount of the filling in the middle of the circle
  6. Grab the edges and bring them together to make a crescent type shape (Make sure to make it so stuff won't leak out)
  7. Repeat until you have used all the dough and filling
  8. Bake for 15 on 220 and then lower to 200 for the last 10 mins
